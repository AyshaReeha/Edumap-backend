	+ **Pandemics:** Dealing with pandemics.
	+ **Terrorism:** Facing terrorism.

	+ **Social inequities:** Addressing social inequities.

	+ **Virtual diplomacy:** Virtual face-to-face negotiations may be as effective as in-person ones.
	+ **Laws governing the metaverse:** Who will govern it? Who will have jurisdiction over what?
